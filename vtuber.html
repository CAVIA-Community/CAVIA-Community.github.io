Becoming a VTuber

Essential Equipment

  • PC/Laptop: Minimum i5/Ryzen 5 processor, 8GB RAM, dedicated GPU recommended
  • Webcam: For face tracking (Logitech C920 or better)
  • Microphone: USB mics like Blue Yeti or XLR setups for better quality
  • VR Headset (optional): For full-body tracking (HTC Vive, Oculus Quest)
  • Lighting: Ring lights or softbox lights for consistent illumination
  • Software: OBS Studio, VTube Studio, Live2D Cubism

Free/Low-Cost Alternatives

  • VRoid Studio: Free 3D model creation software
  • PrprLive: Free 2D tracking software
  • Phone Apps: Some face tracking apps can work as webcam replacements
  • Asset Stores: Purchasing pre-made assets can be cheaper than full custom work
  • Commissioning New Artists: Often more affordable than established artists
